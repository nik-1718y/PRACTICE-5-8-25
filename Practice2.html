Popular Destinations

TajMahal,Agar

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the right bank of the river Yamuna in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India. It was commissioned in 1631 by the fifth Mughal emperor, Shah Jahan, to house the tomb of his beloved wife, Mumtaz Mahal; it also houses the tomb of Shah Jahan himself

Golden Temple,Punjab

The Golden Temple, also known as Sri Harmandir Sahib, is a prominent Sikh gurdwara located in Amritsar, Punjab, India. It is the pre-eminent spiritual site of Sikhism and a major pilgrimage destination for Sikhs worldwide. The temple is renowned for its stunning architecture, particularly the gold foil covering that adorns its upper floors, giving it the name "Golden Temple".

Konark Sun Temple, Odisha

The Konark Sun Temple, located in Odisha, India, is a 13th-century temple dedicated to the Hindu sun god Surya. It's renowned for its unique architectural style, elaborate stone carvings, and its representation as a giant chariot of the sun god, pulled by seven horses. The temple is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a significant example of Kalinga architecture.
